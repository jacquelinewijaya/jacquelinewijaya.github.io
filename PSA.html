PSA

“PSA” is a poster that serves to raise awareness about the pressing issue of food insecurity. It features a picture of utensils that are arranged to represent different parts of the body. It’s been edited to an extreme to portray feelings of heaviness in order to highlight the weight that this issue carries. The poster’s message is a wake-up call to viewers, especially college students who suffer from food insecurity more than people realize.
